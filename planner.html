Rental Information

Liberty High Community Gymnasium is available for rental for a variety of events, including:

  • Sporting Events
  • Community Gatherings
  • Concerts
  • Trade Shows
  • And more!

Rental includes access to the gymnasium, seating areas, and basic amenities. Additional services, such as catering and event setup, can be arranged upon request.
